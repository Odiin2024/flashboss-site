How to Use a Key

Activate your FlashBoss beta key on Steam

  1. Open Steam

    Launch the Steam client on your computer.

  2. Go to Games > Activate a Product on Steam

    In the top menu bar, click Games then select Activate a Product on Steam...

  3. Enter Your Key

    Follow the prompts and paste your beta key when asked.

    Your key will look something like: XXXXX-XXXXX-XXXXX

  4. Download and Play

    FlashBoss will appear in your Steam library. Click Install, and you're ready to begin your studies.
