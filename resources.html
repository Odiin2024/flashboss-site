Every FlashBoss pack is built from two things: a word list and a set of reference lessons. Available here — free to read, free to print, or to use as you like without restriction.

FlashBoss adds curation: Fibonacci-derived spaced repetition for adaptive study sessions, a choice of master guides and difficulty, boss fights, audio, and a structured, distraction-free learning environment.
