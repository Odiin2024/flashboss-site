The Grimoire

Extra practice, earned protection

What Is the Grimoire?

The Grimoire is a magical tome that allows you to do extra practice sessions after completing your daily requirements. Using the Grimoire earns you Drive.

Powerdrive Sessions

After completing your daily streak requirement, the Grimoire becomes available. Each Powerdrive session lets you review additional cards beyond your daily minimum.

Drive: Days Off Earned

When you complete a Grimoire session, you pull time forward by a day within the sanctum. Drive is the mismatch between sanctum time and reality.

Your Drive equals saved-up days off.

If you miss a day of practice, Drive is consumed instead of breaking your streak. This protects dedicated learners who occasionally need a break.

Session Limits

Some masters limit how many Powerdrive sessions you can do per day:

  • Luna, Shen, Aquila: Maximum 3 sessions per day
  • Claude, Odiin, Kitsune: No daily limit

Masters with limits celebrate your dedication but encourage rest. Masters without limits trust you to manage your own pace.

Strategic Use

Build up Drive before vacations, busy periods, or times when you know you won't be able to practice. Your accumulated Drive will protect your streak.
