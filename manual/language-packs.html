Language Packs

Included Packs

FlashBoss comes with the following language packs:

  • German Core: Essential German vocabulary across 5 tiers with full audio pronunciation
  • Esperanto Core: Free introductory Esperanto vocabulary

DLC Packs

Additional content is available as downloadable content (DLC):

  • German Pareto 1: Extended German vocabulary
  • German Pareto 2: Advanced German vocabulary

Installing DLC

DLC packs are installed through Steam. Once purchased, the content will be automatically available in FlashBoss the next time you launch.

Switching Packs

If you have multiple language packs installed, you can switch between them from the Sanctum menu. Each pack tracks progress independently - switching packs won't affect your progress in other languages.

Pack-Specific Progress

Your profile stores separate progress for each language pack:

  • Streak counts are per-pack
  • Drive is per-pack
  • Tier progress is per-pack
  • Boss victories are per-pack

This means you can study multiple languages without them interfering with each other.

Future Languages

More language packs are planned for future releases. Check the Steam store page for announcements.
