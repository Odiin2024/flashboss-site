Mac (Apple Silicon) Setup

You Need Rosetta 2

Your M-series Mac needs Rosetta 2 to run FlashBoss.

Open Terminal and run:

softwareupdate --install-rosetta

Follow the prompts. One-time install, takes a minute.

Already have it? The command will just tell you it's installed.

That's it. Launch FlashBoss from Steam.
