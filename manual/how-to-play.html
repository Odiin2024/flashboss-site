How to Play

A FlashBoss session, beginning to end.

Listen· Read· Repeat· Rate· Fight

Listen
A new word and an example sentence are spoken aloud. The word appears on screen; the sentence does not. First exposure.
Read
The example sentence appears, and beneath it a translation. Built from words you've already learned. You read both, in your own time.
Repeat
Replay the audio as many times as you want. Move on when you're ready.
Rate
Score your recognition from 0 to 5. Be honest — only a 5 advances a card. Two 5s in a row and the card graduates.
Fight

When a cluster is ready, a boss fight begins. Eight multiple-choice answers. Correct, you advance. Wrong, you slide back. Easy cards open the fight; the hardest arrive in the middle, when the stakes are highest.

Miss a hard card and it becomes your easiest next time — waiting for you, in the slot where you most need a win. You can't conquer a cluster until your weakest words are mastered. Try it now.

Each cluster you conquer joins your trophy list.
Vanquish them all. Finish the language.
