Meet the Masters

Each master offers a different difficulty level and teaching style. Choose wisely - your master determines your daily streak requirement and how many new cards you receive each day.

Master Luna (Easy)

Luna came from the wild forests. She is caring, supportive, and firm when needed. With just 8 cards to maintain for your streak, she offers the gentlest path forward. You receive 3 new cards per day.

Best for: Children, casual learners, or those wanting a relaxed experience.

Master Claude (Medium)

Claude emerged from the digital realm. He is methodical, precise, and efficient, believing that systematic learning yields the best results. With 13 cards to maintain for your streak, he provides a balanced challenge. You receive 5 new cards per day.

Best for: Serious learners who want steady progress.

Master Shen (Medium)

Shen arrived from the ancient mountain monasteries. He is strict, traditional, and unwavering in his belief that structure creates strength. With 13 cards to maintain for your streak, he ensures you develop proper learning habits. You receive 5 new cards per day.

Best for: Those who thrive with discipline and structure.

Master Odiin (Challenging)

Odiin has been the guardian of knowledge at the Academy for centuries. He is wise, patient, and analytical, believing that steady progress beats rushed learning. With 21 cards to maintain for your streak, he offers a challenging but sustainable path. You receive 8 new cards per day.

Best for: Committed learners with genuine motivation.

Master Kitsune (Highest)

Kitsune came from the forests of feudal Japan. She is clever, playful, and mischievous, and expects a lot from you. With 34 cards to maintain for your streak, she will not tell you what to do - but she expects results. You receive 13 new cards per day.

Best for: Experienced language learners or those reviewing rusty vocabulary.

Master Aquila (Highest)

Aquila descended from the highest peaks. He is demanding, inspiring, and relentless in his pursuit of excellence. With 34 cards to maintain for your streak, he will push you beyond what you thought possible. You receive 13 new cards per day.

Best for: Those who have already learned multiple languages or want an extreme challenge.

Changing Masters

You can change your master at any time from the Meet the Masters menu in the Sanctum. Your progress is preserved, but your streak requirements will adjust to match your new master.
