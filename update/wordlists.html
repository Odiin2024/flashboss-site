Word Lists

Free, printable vocabulary lists for every FlashBoss language — English, Italian, German, Spanish, and Esperanto. The most useful words in each language, frequency-ranked and grouped into thematic clusters across five tiers, each paired with its English translation. Hide the translations to test yourself, or print a cluster and cut your own flashcards. Free to use however you like. How it works →

Using the word lists

  • Pick a language and set with the Pack and Set menus.
  • Choose clusters: open Clusters, click one, then Shift-click another to take a contiguous range (Ctrl / ⌘-click adds single ones). Click OK, press Enter, or click outside to close.
  • Translations toggle: on shows word + translation; off hides them so you can self-test — and prints word-only cards.
  • Print: the selected clusters print as cut-out, foldable flashcards — word and translation side by side with a centre fold line. Several clusters print together to save paper.

Card borders may not appear in your browser's print preview — they are present on the actual printout.
