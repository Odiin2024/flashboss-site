German core design

The twofold path

Songs of Innocence, and of Experience.

German Core is structured carefully. The first three tiers keep adult concerns out — deliberately. The market, the U-Bahn, a first week's small talk: the Germany you pictured before you arrived, taught straight, without complication.

Unfortunately, this can't last. Germany is a country built for high achievement, and it leaves little wiggle room — not for lateness, not for a missing stamp, and not for small oversights. Germany requires precision. You have 6 months and only 6 months to transfer your drivers license on arrival, or you have to do the full driving test. Be warned.

The later tiers face that squarely. By the end of Core you are no longer reciting phrases; you are reading a rejection notice and noticing you have four weeks to file a Widerspruch.

Tiers 1–3 · Innocence

Greetings, names of things, education, and daily life. Getting started in a new language.

Tiers 4–5 · Experience

Cultural integration and the real machinery — precision, paperwork, and deadline after deadline.

Watch it grow up

Progressively more challenging

The example sentences increase in complexity tier by tier.

  1. Ich bin Student.I am a student.
  2. Ich kaufe frisches Obst auf dem Markt.I buy fresh fruit at the market.
  3. Könnten Sie mir bitte erklären, wo ich einen Fahrschein lösen kann?Could you please explain to me where I can buy a ticket?
  4. Mir liegt es am Herzen, dass wir diese Angelegenheit vertraulich behandeln.It matters deeply to me that we handle this matter confidentially.
  5. Die Implikationen dieser Entscheidung übersteigen bei Weitem unsere ursprünglichen Erwartungen.The implications of this decision far exceed our original expectations.
