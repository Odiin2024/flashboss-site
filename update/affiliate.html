the nuts and bolts

UTM Attribution

Steam credits a sale to your link only when the buyer was signed in to Steam in the browser where they clicked, and purchases within 72 hours.

So you are paid on the sales Steam can prove, not on every sale you influence. You'll get a report of your running total if it doesn't hit the threshold.

How it works

  1. Register for a UTM link.
  2. I confirm by email with your start date. Sales count from that day.
  3. You receive your unique UTM link.
  4. Post it where your audience is — a channel, a forum, a class.
  5. Once Steam settles the month, you're paid within the business week.
